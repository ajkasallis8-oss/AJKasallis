Why I Love the Strength of the Southwest

Living in the Southwest makes it easy to appreciate wide-open views, early-morning desert sunlight, and the value of staying active. Both huge parts in my life I find it important people see both sides of who I am.

Training Priorities

  2. Recover well

    • Stay hydrated
  3. Enjoy the process
    • Train with purpose
    • Pick motivating environments
    • Celebrate small wins
